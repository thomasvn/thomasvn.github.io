About

"Walk with me for a while, my friend - you in my shoes, I in yours - and then let us talk."

- Richelle E. Goodrich



Hi, my name is Thomas and I'm currently a sophomore studying Computer Science and Engineering at Santa Clara University. I'm an aspiring full-stack developer and I'm always hungry to learn more.

In my free time I enjoy getting active which can run from recreational football, to photography adventures. Since I find it hard to describe everything there is about me in a paragraph, below are some fun facts about me that might give you a better idea of who I am!

49ers

Growing up in the Bay Area, I've always been a fan of the 49ers. Though times may be tough now, I have hope for its near future.

Although I never played football in high school, I really enjoy tossing the football around with friends. I hope to join an intramural team when the following season arrives.

Manchester United

Just recently diving into the world of soccer (football), I was swayed to become a Manchester United fan through the encouragement of some friends.

Because of the universal language this sport can speak, often times I kick the ball around with people/students who come from different parts of the world.

Warriors

Although I have always been a fan of the Warriors because they are a Bay Area team, I never truly followed the sport until after I realized its connections in tactics similar soccer.

I enjoy playing pickup games with friends I hope to improve the uncoordinated and awkward syle I currently possess.

Volunteering

Ever since I joined Key Club in high school I have loved giving back to the community in any way I can. As a the Project Coordinator then President of the club, I Organized a wide scope of projects which ranged from mural paintings for the local hospital to crafting blankets for abused children.

Because of these experiences, much of what I do today is with a larger goal to give back to the community, especially with my degree.

Photography

If you haven't checked out my Portfolio section yet, you should know that I love digital photography. I first started back in high school as a yearbook photographer and I've enjoyed it ever since.

Through hectic and stressful times I often enjoy taking a breather by just stepping outside or roaming the city with my friends and my camera.

Cooking

Although I'm no expert yet, I have recently found a certain joy in cooking. I think the enjoyment comes moreso in the idea that this food revolves around people -- how it was introduced in a certain culture, how we can cook the food together, and how we can share the meal together.

I've found that the experiences created in this process have been much more special and worthwhile.
